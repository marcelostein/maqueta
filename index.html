Shri Mataji

Shri Mataji Nirmala Devi is unique in the history of spirituality because she is the first and only one to offer the experience of self-realization en masse. She declared that it is the birthright of all human beings - regardless of religion, race, nationality or circumstance - to receive their Self Realisation.

Sahaja Yoga

Sahaja Yoga meditation is a technique developed by Shri Mataji Nirmala Devi to sustain the awakening experienced through Self Realisation where the individual unites with their true self, or one can say individual consciousness joins with the collective consciousness.
